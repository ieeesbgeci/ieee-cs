IEEE CS SBC GECI

Overview

SBC16681A - Govt Engineering College Idukki, C16

Staff Advisor Dr. Visakh R

In 2020, a group of students headed by Miss Archalakshmy KS put forward the idea of forming the Computer Society in the Student Branch. Soon the groundwork started and the IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter Government Engineering College Idukki got the petition approved on 18 May 2021 (SBC16681A - Govt Engineering College Idukki, C16). IEEE CS SBC GECI was inaugurated on 12 June 2021 by Mr Srinivasan Ravindran, Chairman IEEE CS Kerala Chapter in the presence of dignitaries from Kerala CS Kerala Chapter. We aims to enhance networking, provide a plethora of volunteering opportunities to students, introduce existing technical-professional initiatives and empower the people who advance technology by delivering tools for individuals at all stages of their professional careers. Our chapter activities include Talk sessions & workshops and a series of coding sessions in the name Hour of Code which aims to develop a coding community in our college.
